Huangmei Opera

Huangmei Opera originated as a form of rural folksong and dance that has been in existence for the last 200 years and possibly longer. The original Huangmei opera was sung by women when they were picking tea, thus was called the Picking Tea Song. In the late Qing dynasty, the songs came into Anhui Province and gradually developed into a newborn's operas.
Huangmei means yellow plum flowers.

The Legendary Marriage between fairy and man

In the temple of heaven, the jade emperor's youngest daughter, the seven fairies, and her sisters visited lingxutai. Among them, Dong Yong became a slave to earn money so that he can bury his father. He met the seventh sister of fairies on his way to the landlord's house. The seventh fairy fell in love with him right away, and begged her sisters to help her go down to earth and marry Dong Yong. Her sisters could not resist the entreaty of the Seventh fairy and disobeyed the precepts of the heavenly palace. The Seventh fairy came down to earth with incense and married Dong Yong.
